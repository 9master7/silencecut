About Us

Built for creators,
by a creator.

SilenceCut Pro was born out of frustration with hours wasted editing dead air in audio recordings.

Every podcaster, YouTuber, and voiceover artist knows the pain: you finish a great recording session, then spend hours in an editor removing pauses, breaths, and background noise one by one. SilenceCut Pro automates all of that in seconds.

We built this tool because affordable, powerful audio cleanup should be available to every creator — not just studios with expensive software. Based in India 🇮🇳, we price for Indian creators.
