Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data SilenceCut Pro collects and how we use it.

1. Audio Files

Your audio files are processed securely. Files are temporarily stored during processing only and are automatically deleted within 1 hour. We never access, listen to, or share your audio content.

2. Account Data

When you create an account we collect your name and email address. This is used for login, billing communication, and support.

3. Payment Data

All payments are handled by Razorpay. We never see or store your card number or payment details. Razorpay's privacy policy applies to payment data.

4. Usage Data

We track how many files you process each month to enforce plan limits. We may also collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the product.

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

6. Data Deletion

Email support@silencecutpro.in to request deletion of your account and all associated data. We process requests within 7 business days.

7. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Payment data is handled entirely by Razorpay and never stored on our servers.
